About Us

We are an Organization that researches
the art of yoga and its various practicies.
In indain ancient times,these practicies
known as yoga were not called yoga. They
practiced mainly by ancient sages that
passed their teachingsand these practicies
by word of mouth to anyone was willing to listen.

Today, many people in many other other
countries also practice yoga. However , they
do not know the subtleties of the art.
We aim to change that. We are dedicated to
teaching and showing how yoga can heal
,enrich and energize you. You can take
workshops as individuals or as a family.
